    Savings Plans

    Fixed Savings

    Manage members cash contribution using digitized records, with easy access to pay contributions through payment channels- Card, on flutter waves. Fixed Savings allow you to make a one-time payment after creating your plan

    Continuous Savings

    Manage continuous savings process. This allows you to create a savings' plan. Thereafter, you can pay up your planned amount instamentally. Continuous payments will be via flutterwaves platform using your respective bank cards.
